About Us

Discover Our Company

Established in 1980 by the visionary entrepreneurs Santiago Trivelloni and Julieta Murchio, our company stands as a testament to a rich legacy rooted in the heart of Argentina. Born out of a deep passion for agriculture and a commitment to quality, we have evolved into a premier fruit-producing and exporting enterprise. It was the foresight and dedication have been pivotal in shaping our journey, transforming a humble beginning into a global success story.

Nestled in the fertile landscapes of Argentina, our orchards benefit from the region's unique climate and soil, cultivating a diverse array of fruits renowned for their exceptional taste and nutritional value. Over the decades, we have become synonymous with excellence in fruit cultivation, leveraging sustainable practices and cutting-edge technology to ensure the highest quality produce.

As a prominent exporter, we take pride in sharing the bounty of Argentina's harvest with the world. From the iconic citrus groves to the lush vineyards, our commitment to authenticity and freshness is unwavering. Guided by its founder's enduring vision, we continue to be a trusted name in the global fruit industry, connecting cultures through the joy of wholesome, flavorful fruits from the heart of Argentina.

Why and How we do it

Our business operations extend far beyond the borders of Argentina, as we proudly contribute to the international fruit supply chain. Renowned for our meticulous cultivation practices, we harness the fertile soils and favorable climate of our region to yield premium fruits that meet and exceed global standards.

Our state-of-the-art export facilities stand as a testament to our dedication to delivering freshness and flavor to every corner of the world. Equipped with cutting-edge technology and stringent quality control measures, these facilities ensure that only the finest produce reaches our discerning clientele.

As a company deeply committed to sustainability, we implement eco-friendly practices throughout our supply chain, from cultivation to packaging. This commitment not only aligns with our values but also resonates with partners and consumers who prioritize ethically sourced and environmentally conscious products.

In the ever-evolving landscape of international trade, we continue to thrive, adapting our strategies to meet the demands of a dynamic market. Our unwavering commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction positions us as a trusted leader in the global fruit industry.
